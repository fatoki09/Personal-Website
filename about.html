My Story

Hi! I'm Ramon Miguel Flores, but you can call me RMi (pronounced "army"). I'm from Sacramento, California, and went to Jesuit High School in Carmichael, CA. I'm currently a sophomore at Stanford University in Palo Alto, California and I am majoring in Computer Science with a focus in Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) as well as minoring in Management Science & Engineering.

Outside of computer science, many people know me for my passion for sports. I grew up in a vibrant basketball community and am a DIE HARD Sacramento Kings fan. I've been a competitive swimmer since I was 8, and also love to play basketball, football, and watch St. Louis Rams and Stanford Cardinal football. I'm also a big time youtuber, genuine starbucks addict, Rubik's cube solver, wide reader, and family-oriented man.

Thanks for reading and getting to know a little about who I am. I'm extremely excited for the upcoming years and can't wait to learn more about computer science, and see if the Kings can make the playoffs or if the Cardinal can win the national championship :) If you'd like to take a look at my resume or even just try to get to know me a little better, please shoot me a message here. Thanks for stopping by!
